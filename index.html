Olympus Wallet

Olympus is a full node wallet. This means that unlike light wallets (e.g.Yoroi, Adalite etc.) Olympus downloads a full copy of the Oddysey blockchain and independently validates every transaction in its history. That way you get maximum security and completely trustless operation, without centrally hosted 3rd party servers.
